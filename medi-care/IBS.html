Definition

    Irritable bowel syndrome is characterized by recurrent abdominal pain associated with abnormal defaecations in the absence of any structural abnormality of the gut

Causes

  • Unknown
  • Affective disorder
  • Psychological factors
  • Antibiotic therapy
  • Eating disorder
  • IBS with diarrhoea
  • Mixed-alternate constipation
  • Dietary factors-lactose,fructose containing diet maybe responsible

  • Barium enema
  • CT scan of abdomen
  • Thyroid functions test,if necessary

  • Avoid stress,depression
