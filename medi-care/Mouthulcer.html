Medi-care Back
    It is characterized by superficial,painful,round or ovoid ulcer in any part of the mouth,usually recurrent

Causes

  • Unknown,common in young femlae,prior to menstruction and nonsmokers
  • Single or multiple round or oval ulcers with inflammatory hallo often painful
  • May be associated with inflammatory bowel disease,emotional stress

Treatment

  • Avoid Trauma,tobacco,spicy and hot food
  • Oral hygiene
  • Chlorhexidine 0.2% mouth wash
  • Tetracycline mouth wash
  • Sometimes dapsone,colchicine,thalidomide,azathioprine may be used
  • Pain maybe relieved by using local anaesthetic mouth wash
