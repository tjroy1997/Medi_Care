Definition

    ALcohol becomes harmful when taken more than 21 units in men and 14 units in women weekly.There are be social,phychological and physical problem with excessive use of alcohol.

Signs

  • Emotion and behaviour disturbance.
  • Aspiration of vomitus.
  • Respiratory depression.
  • Accident,injuries.

Treatment

  • Corrution of water and electrolytes.
  • High-dose B complex.
  • Sedative-diazepam may be given.
