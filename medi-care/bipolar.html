causes

It is characterised by relapsing mood disturbance with period of elevated mood known as mania and depressive mood

Signs

  • The patient may be cheerful,speaks fluently and over talkative.
  • Inappropriate behaviour, social binding is completely lost.
  • sleep-less,claiming to have many tasks.
  • In sign-impaired,hallucination may be present.

Tratment

  • Patient and family member should be education about relapsing nature and measure to prevent further episode.
  • Antipsychotics-dibenzodiazepines,phenothiazine or butyrophenones
