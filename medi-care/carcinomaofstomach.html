Sites

  • Cardiac end of stomach

Causes

  • diet: preservaions in diet,nitrities which are converted to N-nitroso compunds which are carcinogenic
  • smoking
  • Alcohol
  • Gastric surgery
  • Rarely
    Investigation
  • Hb%,TC,DC,and ESR
  • Barium meal double contrast-shows filling defect,irregular ulcer,in infiltrating type
  • Endoscopy and biopsy
  • Stool for occult blood test
  • To monitor recurrence-carcinoembryonic
    Treatment
  • Surgery is the only curative treatment. 5 year survival is 90% if surgery is done in early gastric cancer,but only 10% if done in advanced cases.
  • Perioperative chemotherapy -ECF
  • Chemotherapy not much helpful. FAM(combination of 5-flurouracil,adrimycin,mito-mycin)
  • palliative-radiotherapy-very little role
  • Endoscopic dilatation
