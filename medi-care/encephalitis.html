Medi-care Back
    Defination: It is the inflammation of brain parenchyma

Causes

  • Herpes simple type I
  • Enterovirus-polio,cocsackie,echo virus
  • Mumps virus
  • Influenza virus
  • Toga virus
  • HIV
  • Lymphocytic chriomeningitis

Signs/Symtoms/h2>
  • Fever,headche,nausea,vomiting,photophobia
  • Drowsiness,impaired consciousness even coma
  • Convulsion
  • Focal neurological sign
  • Abnormal behavior,agitation,hallucination

Investigations

  • CT scan or MRI shows area of odema,low-density lesion in temporal lobe in herps simplex encephalitis
  • Lumber puncture and CSF study- shows clear fluid ,high lymphocyte ,glucose is normal but protein maybe slightly high
  • Viral serology in blood and CSF
  • EEG

Treatment

  • IV acyclovir 10mg/kg 8 hourly for 2-3 weeks
  • IV dexamethasone-6-8 hourly
  • Others-IV fluid,nutrition,care of eyes,bowel,bladder
