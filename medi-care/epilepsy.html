Defination

    It means convulsion caused by abnormal,excessive or synchronous discharge of cerebral neurons. Epilepsy is characterised by recurrent and paroxysmal attack of convulsion or seizure

Causes

  • Idopathic (unknown cause)
  • Traumatic
  • Vascular
  • infection-meningitis,encephalitis,cerebral abscess,cerebral malaria,tuberculosis,HIV.
  • Metabolic
  • Congenitial

Classification

    Generalised
  • Generalised tonic-clonic seizure
  • Absence seizure
  • Typical absence
  • Tonic and atonic seizure
    Partial or focal
  • Simple partial
  • complex partial
  • unclassifiable seizure

Treatment

  • Better to be treated in ICU
  • Oxygen inhalation,airway should be kept clear
  • IV channel
  • Initially injection diazepam 10mg iv over 5 min
  • If seizures presists over 90 minutes -intubation,ventilation and general anaesthesia
