>Defination

It is defined as defect in either initiation or conduction of cardiac impulse

Sites of heart block

  • SA node
  • AV node
  • Bundle of His
  • Branches of bundle of his(left and right)

Types of heart block

  • SA block
  • Atrioventricular block-it is of 3 types ,(a) 1st degree,(b) 2nd degree,(c)3rd degree
  • Bundle branch block. Right bundle branch,left bundle brach

First degree AV block causes

  • Normally found in athletes(due to increased vagal tone)
  • Drugs
  • Acute myocardial infraction
  • Acute rheumatic carditis
  • In elderly (atherosclerosis)
  • Hyperkalaemia

Second degree heart block causes

  • Physiological-in athlete,during rest,sleep
  • Drugs
  • Acute myocardial infraction

Signs

  • Usually,asymptomatic
  • Features of primary disease
  • pulse irregular

Treatment for complete heart block

  • If the patient is symptomatic -permanent pacemaker
  • In congenital complete heart block,pulse rate is high,no pacemaker is necessary

Prevention of stroke

  • Avoid excessive cardio excercise
  • Avoid junk food
