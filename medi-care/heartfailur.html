    Defination :It is the failur of the heart to maintain adequate cardiac output to meen the demand of the tissue or can do so only at the expense of an elevated filling pressure

Types

  • left-sided heart failure
  • Right sided heart failure
  • Biventricular failure

Acute left ventricular failure (LVF)

    Causes
  • Systemic hypertension
  • Acute myocardial infarction
  • Aortic valvular disease
  • Mitral regurgitation
  • Cardiomyopathy
  • Coarctation aorta
  • Rapid or excess infusion of fluid or blood or plasma

Symptoms

  • Breathlessness,may be orthopnoea
  • Cough with frothy sputum,occasional haemoptysis
  • Palpitation
  • FFeatures of low cardiac output-restlessness,sweating,oliguria

signs

  • Patient looks dyspnoeic with propped up position
  • Cyanosis
  • Pulse-tacycardia,may be pulsus alternans
  • Blood pressure low but may be high if the patient is hypertensive

Signs

  • Patient may be semi or unconscious
  • Slurring of speech may be present
  • Sensory system may be intact
  • reduction of muscles power

Treatment (LVF)

  • Bed rest
  • Propped up position
  • High flow oxygen inhalation (600-100%)
  • Diuretic frusemide iV 80-120mg, may be repeated
  • ACE inhibitor -ramipril,captopril,enalapril
  • If no response,inotropic agents like dopamine,dobutamine may be added
  • Treatment of primary cause
  • Antiarrythmic drug

Prevention of Heart attack

  • Healthy lifestyle
  • no smoking ,no wine
  • Less oil in meal
  • Excercise daily
