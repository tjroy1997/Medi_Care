    Definition:It is the herniation of part of the stomach into the chest through the oesophageal hiatus of diaphragm

types-3types

  • Rolling or paraoesophageal
  • Skiding or oesophagogastric
  • Rarely,a mixed type

Clinical features

  • May be asymptomatic
  • Heart burn,regurgitation of food
  • Retrosternal chest pain
  • Dysphagia,if oesophageal stricture develops
    Investigation
  • Barium swallow-X ray of oesophagus in trendelenburg position
  • Endoscopy
    Treatment
  • As in GERD
  • Surgery-if symptoms persist despite adequate medical therapy
