Defination

    :Leukaemia is a malignant disorder of haemopoietic stem cell characterised by increased number of primitive WBC in marrow and peripheral blood.

Acute leukemia Symptoms

  • Features of anaemia—weakness, dizziness, giddiness.
  • fever,malaise
  • Bleeding-nasal,gum,persistent bleeding after operation
  • Infection
  • Pain in bones and joint
    signs
  • anemia
  • bruise,ecchymosis,petechiae
  • Bony tenderness
  • Hepatosplenomegaly
    Investigation
  • CBC and PBF—WBC is high, Hb and platelet are low. PBF shows blast cell (In ALL—mostly lymphoblast, in AML—myeloblast)
  • Acquired-autoimmune,toxic,mechanical and infectious causes
  • Bone marrow—hypercellular with leukaemic blast cell.y

Treatment

  • General measures—correction of anaemia, control of infection by antibiotics, control of bleeding, psychological support
  • Specific—by chemotherapy. – Induction of remission. – Consolidation. – Maintenance.
  • Bone marrow transplantation—either allogenic or autologous may be done.
