Causes

It is a systemic viral disease caused by measles virus,transmitted by infected droplet

Treatment

  • Bed rest and isolation until 5 days after the rash has appeared
  • paracetamol for fever
  • Gammaglobulin 0.25 ml/kg can modify the course of disease in immunocompro patient

signs

  • prodromal symptoms or catarrhal phase:Fever ,headache,sneezing,running nose,dry cough,redness of eyes
  • Exanthematous stage:Red maculopapular skin rash ,disappear develops after 3-4 days of prodromalphase

Prevention

  • Live attenuated measles vaccine
