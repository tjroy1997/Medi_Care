Causes

The feature are due to different toxins by different snakes like haematotoxin,cardiotoxin neurotoxin,myotoxin,nephrotoxin and allergic toxin.

clinical feature

  • Local feature-there may be fang marks,pain,numbness,tingling,redness,necrosis.
  • General-nausea,vomiting,headache,fever
  • Muscular-weakness and paralysis.
  • Russel and scaled vipers causes severe local symptoms and haemorrhagic tendency
  • Cobra and krait causes conses constitution symptoms more than local symptoms .

Treatment

  • Maintenance of airway,breathing and circulation
  • local care of wound.
  • Iv fluid,maintenace of nutrition.
  • Antihistamine in some cases
  • Management of complication if any
