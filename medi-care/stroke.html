    Defination: A stroke occurs when the blood supply to part of your brain is interrupted or severely reduced depriving brain tissue of oxigen and nutrients .A stroke is a medical emergency.Early action can minimize brain damage and potential complications Stroke may be defined as sudden development of focal neurogical deficit due to nontraumatic vascular cause

signs

  • patient may be unconscious
  • slurrring of speech may be persent
  • motor system(signs of upper motor neurone lesion)-usuallly hemiplegia
  • sensory system-may be intact

    • Causes

    • Transient ischaemic attack (TIA) sudden neurological dysfunction due to ceberal ischaemia lasting less than 24 hours
    • Vascular Headache -occurs in migraine,cluster headache,
    • High blood pressure
    • Tobacco. Smoking or chewing it raises your odds of a stroke. Nicotine makes your blood pressure go up.
    • Heart disease. This condition includes defective heart valves as well as atrial fibrillation, or irregular heartbeat, which causes a quarter of all strokes among the very elderly. You can also have clogged arteries from fatty deposits
    • Diabetes. People who have it often have high blood pressure and are more likely to be overweight.

    Diseases included in CVA

    • Cerebral haemorrhage
    • Cerebral thrombosis
    • Cerebral embolism

    Risk Factors in CVD

    • More in male
    • Genetics
    • Obesity,lifestyle
    • Heart Disease
    • Oral contraceptive pill

    Clinical features (symptoms)

    • weakness of one side of the body
    • difficulty in speech and swallowing
    • Urinary incontinence
    • Semi or unconsciousness

    Signs

    • Patient may be semi or unconscious
    • Slurring of speech may be present
    • Sensory system may be intact
    • reduction of muscles power

    Treatment

    • Oropharyngeal suction
    • I/V channel
    • NG tube feeding
    • Maintenance of nutritional status
    • speech therapy
    • Physiotherapy
    • Occupational therapy

    Prevention of stroke

    • Risk factors like hypertension,diabetes mellitus,obesity etc should be controlled.
    • smoking and alchohol should be stopped
    • Regular Physical excercise
    • Antiplatelet drug
